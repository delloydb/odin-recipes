friut salad

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seeded watermelon
  • 2 cups sliced fresh strawberries
  • 2 large fresh peaches, cut into cubes
  • 2 large nectarines, cut into cubes
  • 1 red Anjou pear, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup seedless grapes, halved
  • 2 lemons, juiced
  • ¼ cup minced fresh mint (chocolate mint preferred)
  • ½ lemon, zested
  • 1 tablespoon honey (fireweed honey preferred)

Steps to follow

  1. Combine watermelon, strawberries, peaches, nectarines, pear, and grapes in a large mixing bowl.
  2. Whisk lemon juice, mint, lemon zest, and honey together in a small bowl; drizzle over the fruit mixture and toss to coat.
  3. For best results, refrigerate the fruit salad for 1 hour before serving.
  4. Enjoy!
